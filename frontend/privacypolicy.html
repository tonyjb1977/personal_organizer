Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Personal Organizer web application (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our personal organizer tool at https://personal-organizer.example.com. By accessing or using the application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve your experience:

Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account password
  • Task details (titles, descriptions, due dates)

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage logs (pages visited, actions taken, timestamps)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the application
  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync and display your to-do items and reminders
  • Send you notifications, updates, and security alerts
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and performance

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

With Service Providers

We share data with partners who help us:

  • Host and maintain infrastructure
  • Deliver emails
  • Provide analytics

They are authorized only to perform services on our behalf and must keep your data confidential.

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, to enforce our Terms of Service, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking tools to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Personalize your interface (theme, layout)
  • Gather anonymous analytics to understand usage trends

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain features of the application.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure communication using HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
  • Passwords stored using strong hashing algorithms

However, no system is entirely foolproof. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access and Update: Log into your account to view or edit your profile and tasks.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time; we will remove your personal data except as required to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.
  • Email Preferences: Opt out of non-essential emails by clicking “unsubscribe” in any message.

Children’s Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us to request deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting the new policy on our site and updating the “Effective Date” below. Your continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
